Summary: Keri's API Weather Project

Welcome! This website is all about analyzing how the weather changes as you get closer to the equator. Now, I know you're thinking that of course it gets hotter the closer you get to the equator. That's true. Yet, I want to prove it to you and show you a couple of other cool things while I'm at it. To accomplish this analysis, I first pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator.

I used Matplotlib to plot various graphs analyzing the city data. The factors I looked at include: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. Throughout my site you will find four graphs and their explanations. Enjoy and thank you for visiting!
